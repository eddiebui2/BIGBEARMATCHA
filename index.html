Our Story

Born from a love of good mornings

Big Bear Matcha started as a tiny pop-up in Edmonton with one matcha bowl and a big dream. We wanted to make ceremonial-grade matcha approachable, fun, and just a little bit wild.

Today we're a full neighbourhood café — but the mission is the same: brew something worth waking up for, every single day.
